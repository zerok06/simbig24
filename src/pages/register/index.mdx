# Registration process
1. For **foreign participants**, make a **USD transfer** using the information above. For **Peruvian participants**, please go to the Banco de la Nacion agency and make a **deposit** (in USD or Soles) using the information above. Please **keep the transfer receipt** as it will be required to obtain the **SIMBig 2024 badge**.
2. **Fill in the registration form** by clicking on the button below. The form contains the bank transfer information. A **digital copy (photo)** of the transfer/payment will also be required.
3. A **confirmation email** will be sent to complete the registration process.

**Important notice**: Bank fees will be at the expense of the participant.
